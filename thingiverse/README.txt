# Summary

Inspired by [Dog Rail Clips](https://tsoproducts.com/workholding-accessories/ujk-technology-dog-rail-clips/), here's a fully printable clip that works for the Triton tracks.

This is likely compatible with the Makita tracks.  Festool owners can just buy the UJK part.

The design assumes your cutting surface is above the 10mm base of the Parf Dogs as the spring clips only can reliably go around the 20mm diameter section of the posts.  

# Print Settings

Printer Brand: Creality
Printer: Ender 3
Rafts: Yes
Supports: No
Resolution: 0.2
Infill: 20
Filament_brand: AmazonBasics
Filament_material: PETG

Notes: 
I printed the Rail Dog with the raft just so it doesn't get knocked off my plate.  The Spring Clip however can just use a simple brim.

The a chamfer on the Rail Dog is to minimize cleanup.  It avoids elephant foots from interfering with the rail.
